6.2.  Removing Tabs

Figure 11.31.  A dialog in a dock, with the “Close Tab” button highlighted


If you want to remove a dialog from a dock, there are two ways you can do it. First, if you click on the drag handle area and drag the dialog away, releasing it someplace other than a docking bar, it will form a new dock in its own right. Second, clicking on the “Close Tab” button (highlighted in the figure to the right) will close the front-most dialog.
