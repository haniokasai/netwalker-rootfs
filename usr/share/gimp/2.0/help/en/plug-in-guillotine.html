9.10.  Guillotine

Revision History
Revision $Revision: 2406 $ 2006-08-10 scb

The Guillotine command slices up the current image, based on the image's guides. It cuts the image along each guide, similar to slicing documents in an office with a guillotine (paper cutter) and creates new images out of the pieces. For further information on guides, see the glossary entry for Guides.

9.10.1.  Activate the Command

  • You can access this command from the image menubar through Image → Transform → Guillotine.
