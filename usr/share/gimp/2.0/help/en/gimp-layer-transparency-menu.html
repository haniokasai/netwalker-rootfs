Figure 15.118.  The “Transparency” submenu of the “Layer” menu


The Transparency submenu contains commands which use or affect the alpha channel of the active layer.

10.31.1.  Activating the Submenu

  • You can access this submenu from the image menubar through Layer → Transparency.

10.31.2.  The Contents of the “Transparency” Submenu

The Transparency submenu contains the following commands:

  • Section 10.32, “ Add Alpha Channel ”

  • Section 10.33, “ Color to Alpha ”

  • Section 10.34, “ Semi-flatten ”

  • Section 10.35, “ Threshold Alpha ”

  • Section 10.36, “ Alpha to Selection ”

  • Section 10.37, “ Add Alpha channel to Selection ”

  • Section 10.38, “ Subtract from Selection ”

  • Section 10.39, “ Intersect Alpha channel with Selection ”
